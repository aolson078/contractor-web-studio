# Templates and Hooks

Use placeholders as scaffolding, then replace them with specific details, proof, and brand voice.

## Use LinkedIn Templates

### Use the Story Post

```text
[Hook with surprise, tension, or lesson]

[Set the scene]

[Name the challenge]

[Explain what happened]

[Show the turning point]

[Share the result]

[State the lesson]

[Ask a question]
```

### Use the Contrarian Take

```text
[State the unpopular opinion]

Here is why:

[Reason 1]
[Reason 2]
[Reason 3]

[Recommend the better path]

[Invite disagreement or discussion]
```

### Use the List Post

```text
[Number] things I learned about [topic] after [credibility marker]:

1. [Point] — [Brief explanation]
2. [Point] — [Brief explanation]
3. [Point] — [Brief explanation]

[Wrap-up insight]

Which point matters most to you?
```

### Use the How-To Post

```text
How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]:

Step 1: [Action]
Why it matters: [Reason]

Step 2: [Action]
Why it matters: [Reason]

Step 3: [Action]
Avoid this mistake: [Warning]

[State expected result]

[Add CTA or question]
```

## Use Twitter/X Thread Templates

### Use the Tutorial Thread

```text
Tweet 1: [Hook + promise]
Here is exactly how to [outcome]:

Tweet 2-7: [One step or idea per tweet]

Final tweet: [Summary + CTA]
```

### Use the Story Thread

```text
Tweet 1: [Intriguing setup]

Tweet 2-6: [Story beats that increase tension]

Tweet 7: [Resolution + lesson]

Final tweet: [Takeaway + follow CTA]
```

### Use the Breakdown Thread

```text
Tweet 1: [Company/person] just [did thing].
Here is why it matters:

Tweet 2-6: [Analysis points]

Final tweet: [Key takeaway + CTA]
```

## Use Instagram Structures

### Use the Carousel Outline

```text
Slide 1: [Bold statement or sharp question]
Slide 2-9: [One clear point per slide]
Slide 10: [Summary + CTA]

Caption: [Expand the lesson, add context, prompt action]
```

### Use the Reel or Short-Form Script

```text
Hook (0-2 sec): [Pattern interrupt or bold claim]
Setup (2-5 sec): [Context]
Value (5-25 sec): [Advice, story, or breakdown]
CTA (25-30 sec): [Comment, follow, share, or click]
```

## Use Hook Formulas

### Use Curiosity Hooks

- I was wrong about [belief].
- The real reason [outcome] happens is not what people think.
- [Result] only took [surprisingly short time].
- Nobody talks about [insider detail].

### Use Story Hooks

- Last week, [unexpected event] happened.
- I almost [major mistake].
- [Time] ago, I was [old state]. Today, I am [new state].
- [Person] told me something I still use every week.

### Use Value Hooks

- How to [outcome] without [pain].
- [Number] mistakes that [negative outcome].
- The simplest way to [outcome].
- Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead.

### Use Contrarian Hooks

- Unpopular opinion: [statement].
- [Common advice] is wrong. Here is why.
- I stopped [common practice] and [positive result] happened.
- Everyone says [X]. The truth is [Y].

### Use Social Proof Hooks

- We [achieved result] in [timeframe]. Here is how.
- [Number] people asked me about [topic]. Here is the answer.
- [Authority] taught me [lesson].

## Use CTA Patterns

- Ask for agreement or disagreement.
- Ask which point resonated most.
- Ask the reader to save, share, or send it to someone.
- Ask for a keyword comment when a follow-up asset exists.
- Ask for a DM only when the intent is genuinely high-value.
