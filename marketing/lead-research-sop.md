# Lead Research SOP

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Build a clean weekly list of 30 local contractor leads that match the ICP and show visible digital weakness worth contacting.

## Weekly Target

- 30 researched and scored leads
- 20-25 contacted
- 5-10 held for later if score is high but timing is unclear

## Sources

Use these in order:

1. Google Maps and Google Business Profile results
2. local directories
3. company websites
4. company Facebook pages
5. local contractor association or business listings

## Research Steps

1. search by city and priority trade
2. open the business website and GBP
3. check for obvious site and trust issues
4. confirm the business looks active
5. collect contact and context fields
6. score the lead
7. tag it for city and trade

## Fast Qualification Questions

- Is the business in Winston-Salem or Greensboro?
- Is the trade in the first-wave focus?
- Does the site look weaker than the business likely is?
- Is there at least one obvious issue worth mentioning?
- Does the business look active enough to invest?

If the answer to most is yes, keep the lead.

## Evidence Of Active Business

- fresh reviews
- recent photos
- active phone number
- active social or recent work posts
- clear service coverage

## Do Not Add

- businesses outside the local-first scope without a very strong fit
- companies already running a strong marketing machine
- lead records without a specific personalization angle
- any record missing enough contact context to act on

## Handoff To Outbound

Each accepted lead should move into the outbound sheet with:

- company
- city
- trade
- website
- best contact path
- one clear issue
- score
- status = Prospect

Use the column schema in `assets/lead-sheet-column-spec.md`.
