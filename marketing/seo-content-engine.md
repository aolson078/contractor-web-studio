# SEO and Content Engine

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Objective

Create a local SEO and conversion content system that supports trust, call generation, and city/trade relevance without publishing thin pages.

SEO should compound in parallel with outbound and referrals. It is not the primary source of calls in the first 30-45 days.

## Initial Scope

### Primary cities

- Winston-Salem
- Greensboro

### Secondary city

- High Point

### Primary trades

- masonry / hardscaping
- deck / fence

### Secondary trade

- concrete

## Page Build Order

Build in this exact order:

1. masonry / hardscaping website design
2. deck / fence website design
3. concrete contractor website design
4. contractor website designer Winston-Salem
5. contractor website designer Greensboro
6. website pricing for contractors
7. custom-coded contractor websites vs Wix
8. custom-coded contractor websites vs WordPress
9. supporting blog posts

## Core Page Types

- `[trade] website design`
- `contractor website designer [city]`
- `custom-coded contractor websites vs Wix`
- `custom-coded contractor websites vs WordPress`
- `website pricing for contractors`

## CTA Rules

Every page must drive to one of only these three CTAs:

- Get a Free Quote
- Book a 15-Min Call
- See My Work

No CTA sprawl.

## Internal Linking Rules

- trade pages -> relevant city pages
- city pages -> portfolio / proof pages
- comparison pages -> offer and pricing pages
- pricing guide -> quote and call CTA
- blog posts -> relevant trade page or city page, not just the home page

## Quality Rules For City and Trade Pages

Never publish a page that exists only because of keyword swapping.

Each page must include:

- trade-specific buying concerns
- city or local service-area context
- distinct FAQs
- unique examples of website problems in that segment
- a clear reason the page exists separately

If a page cannot be made distinct, do not publish it.

## Money Page Briefs

### Masonry / Hardscaping Page

Focus:

- visual proof and project showcase structure
- trust for larger homeowner-ticket projects
- gallery, reviews, and service clarity

### Deck / Fence Page

Focus:

- project photos and before/after logic
- service packages and design/build trust
- strong mobile CTA placement

### Concrete Page

Focus:

- project legitimacy and reliability
- local service clarity
- fast quote path and proof structure

### Winston-Salem and Greensboro City Pages

Focus:

- local positioning
- contractor-specific web design credibility
- why local contractors in that city lose calls today
- relevant nearby proof and local trust framing

## Publishing Cadence

- one money page or comparison page per week until the first 8 pages are live
- one supporting post per week
- one proof or teardown asset repurposed into LinkedIn every week

## On-Page Rules

- title and H1 must match the page intent
- first paragraph must answer the page intent directly
- phone, quote, and portfolio paths must be visible without hunting
- proof must appear above the fold or in the first major section
- FAQs must reflect real contractor objections

## Success Signals

- branded and non-branded organic impressions rise in Search Console
- money pages become entry pages for calls and form submissions
- city or trade pages show clear assisted conversion behavior
