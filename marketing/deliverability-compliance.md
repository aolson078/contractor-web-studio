# Deliverability and Compliance

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Protect the main brand domain while running a lean outbound motion that still gets inboxed and stays manageable.

## Sending Setup

- use the main domain for site, inbound forms, and core brand email
- use a separate outbound sending domain or closely aligned alternative domain for cold outreach
- configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC before sending
- keep outbound plain-text and low-volume

Do not send cold outreach from the same mailbox setup that handles core site notifications.

## Warm-Up Rules

Week 1:

- 5-10 new sends per day

Week 2:

- 10-15 per day

Week 3:

- 15-25 per day

Only increase if bounce rate, spam signals, and reply quality stay healthy.

## List Quality Rules

- do not blast every lead you can find
- contact only qualified local prospects
- prefer visible business emails or verified contact paths
- avoid personal addresses scraped without confidence

## Copy Rules

- plain text only
- one simple CTA
- no images
- no attachment in cold send
- no fake reply or fake forward tricks

## Compliance Rules

- honor opt-outs immediately
- stop after the 4th touch without response
- do not hide identity
- keep the message relevant and local

## Monitoring

Watch weekly:

- bounce rate
- positive reply rate
- overall reply rate
- spam complaints
- manual unsubscribe count

If deliverability weakens, reduce volume before changing the whole strategy.
