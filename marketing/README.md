# Autonomous NC Triad Lead Engine

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Objective

Build a lean, local-first marketing and lead-generation system for Contractor Web Studio that produces a repeatable flow of qualified contractor leads in the NC Triad.

The system should:

- support a 90-day target of 2-4 signed clients per month
- combine partner referrals, audit-based outbound, and local SEO
- automate everything up to booked call and proposal handoff
- keep attribution, proof, and follow-up discipline tight

## Focus

- Market: NC Triad first
- Primary cities: Winston-Salem, Greensboro
- Secondary expansion city: High Point
- Primary trades: masonry/hardscaping, deck/fence
- Secondary trade: concrete

## Non-Negotiables

- Local-first wins. Do not expand geography until one or two trades clearly outperform.
- Narrow before expanding. Start with 2 primary trades and 2 primary cities only.
- Early revenue comes from outbound and referrals. SEO compounds in parallel.
- Proof must be strict. Use only approved testimonials, real work, real screenshots, and grounded case studies.
- Automation stops at booked call. Sales calls, proposal judgment, and closing stay human.
- Every lead must be attributable. No orphan leads, no missing source, no missing next action.

## 90-Day Cadence

### Days 1-14: Foundation and Launch Prep

- finalize ICP, city/trade scope, and offer architecture
- set up tracking, source taxonomy, and lead sheet/CRM structure
- prepare sender and outbound deliverability setup
- create one teardown/audit template
- create one partner referral framework
- tighten website CTA paths and tracking
- publish the first essential pages

### Days 15-30: Early Pipeline Generation

- research 30 qualified leads per week
- contact 20-25 leads per week
- send selective audits
- begin partner outreach
- refine offer language from replies and calls
- publish only the highest-value supporting pages and posts

### Days 31-60: Compounding Assets and Refinement

- expand local landing pages
- publish comparison pages and pricing guide
- improve internal linking and site conversion paths
- collect stronger proof and testimonials
- refine outbound sequences based on reply data

### Days 61-90: Scale What Works

- expand only the best-performing trade
- expand only the best-performing city
- deepen the partner relationships that actually refer
- reduce low-performing motions
- standardize the weekly operating rhythm

## Weekly Operating Checklist

- review dashboard and answer the weekly review questions
- research and score 30 leads
- send 20-25 outbound touches
- send follow-ups to open outbound threads
- contact new and existing partners
- publish or update one high-intent page
- publish one supporting post or proof asset
- clear all open leads without a next action
- review response time compliance
- decide what to double down on, pause, or kill

## Final Execution Rule

Do not try to fully build the machine before running it.

Launch the smallest version that supports:

- clear positioning
- tracked lead flow
- outbound and partner motion
- a few strong pages
- disciplined weekly review

Then scale only what proves itself.
