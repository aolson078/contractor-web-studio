# Partner Engine

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Create a narrow, high-trust referral system with local professionals who already serve the same contractor owners and routinely see weak websites in the wild.

## Initial Partner Categories

Start with only two partner types:

### Visual / brand-adjacent partner

- local photographers
- sign and print shops
- brand support providers with contractor clients

### Trusted advisor / business support partner

- bookkeepers
- virtual assistants
- office admins or business support providers serving contractors

Do not expand partner types until a simple process exists and at least one category is producing signal.

## Partner Fit

Good partners:

- already serve local contractors
- notice weak digital presence regularly
- have a reputation for being trusted, not spammy
- can make warm intros or light referrals without operational pain

## Recommended Referral Structure

Default rule:

- give each partner a dedicated referral link or form
- confirm every referred lead quickly
- pay a flat `$250` referral reward after the referred client pays the initial deposit

If cash rewards are awkward for the relationship, use reciprocal referrals instead.

## Partner Workflow

1. identify 10-15 local partners
2. start with 5 high-fit partners
3. outreach with a simple local-contractor angle
4. give each interested partner one link and one sentence to use
5. confirm received referrals within one business day
6. report outcomes back when appropriate

## Scripts

### First outreach

Hi {{name}},

I run Contractor Web Studio here in the Triad. I help owner-led contractors who do strong work but have weak websites or weak lead capture.

I thought of you because you already work with the same kinds of businesses and probably see that problem often.

If useful, I would love to set up a simple referral arrangement. Nothing complicated - just an easy handoff when you run into a contractor whose site is clearly holding them back.

Open to a quick chat?

### Follow-up

Wanted to circle back on the simple contractor referral idea.

The goal is straightforward: if you meet a local contractor whose online presence is weaker than their real business, you can send them my way and I will handle the rest quickly and professionally.

### Referral acknowledgment

Thanks for sending {{lead_name}} over. I have them in the system and will follow up fast.

I will keep you posted when there is a meaningful outcome.

### Post-referral thank you

Quick update: your referral moved to {{stage_or_outcome}}.

Thanks again for the intro - exactly the kind of contractor fit I am trying to help.

### Reactivation

Checking in - if you run into any contractor clients who clearly need a stronger site or easier quote flow, send them over. I am still handling NC Triad contractor builds and keeping the process simple.

## Follow-Up Cadence

- initial contact
- follow-up 1 at 5-7 days
- follow-up 2 at 10-14 days
- light check-in once per month if warm

## Tracking Rules

Every partner lead must include:

- partner name
- source = partner_referral
- referral date
- stage
- outcome
- reward status

No partner lead should enter the system without attribution.
