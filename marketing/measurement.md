# Measurement

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Measure enough to make good decisions without building a bloated reporting system.

## Leading Indicators

Track weekly:

- qualified leads researched
- outbound messages sent
- positive reply rate
- overall reply rate
- audits delivered
- partner outreaches sent
- partner meetings booked
- pages published
- inbound leads by source
- booked calls by source
- attribution completeness

## Funnel Metrics

Track:

- contact -> reply
- reply -> qualified
- qualified -> audit sent
- audit sent -> call booked
- call booked -> proposal
- proposal -> close

## Lagging Metrics

Track:

- clients signed
- monthly revenue created
- close rate
- average deal value
- best-performing source
- best-performing trade
- best-performing city

## Weekly Review Questions

- which channel produced the most qualified conversations?
- which trade is responding best?
- which city is producing the strongest signal?
- which outreach messages are getting replies?
- which pages are generating calls?
- where are leads getting stuck in the funnel?
- what should be doubled down on next week?
- what should be paused or removed?

## 30 / 60 / 90-Day Targets

### Day 30

- 3-5 booked calls per month pace
- first clear response signals by trade and city
- at least one working outreach sequence
- attribution working end-to-end
- first partner conversations active

### Day 60

- 8-12 qualified opportunities total
- early proof assets in place
- first meaningful SEO and local page base published
- at least one functioning referral source
- consistent pipeline reporting

### Day 90

- 2-4 signed clients per month
- at least 90% of leads have source and stage populated
- median inbound response time under 15 minutes
- one or two trades clearly outperform
- one or two cities justify deeper investment

## Reporting Views

The weekly dashboard should clearly show:

- which pages generate calls
- which templates generate replies
- which partners generate leads
- which trades generate opportunities
- which cities generate wins
