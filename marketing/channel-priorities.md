# Channel Priorities

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Scoring Method

Marketing Feasibility Score:

`MFS = (Impact + Fit + Speed) - (Effort + Cost)`

Higher is better. Only channels with positive and practical fit should be executed.

## Do Now

| Channel | MFS | Role | Why it fits now | Primary KPI |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| Partner / referral engine | +10 | high-trust local demand | Fastest trust transfer, low cost, highly local, fits small service business | referred calls booked |
| Audit-based cold outbound | +9 | fastest direct pipeline | Short time to signal, clear value hook, easy to target weak sites | positive replies / booked calls |
| Local SEO trade and city pages | +9 | compounding inbound | High fit for local contractor intent, strengthens all other channels | organic leads / page-assisted calls |
| Google Business Profile / local presence | +8 | local trust and discovery | Supports map trust, branded search, and credibility | local actions / branded leads |
| Proof and case-study content | +8 | conversion support | Makes all channels close easier | proposal conversion rate |
| Founder-led LinkedIn repurposing | +7 | trust amplifier | Good secondary trust channel, not primary demand engine | profile visits / assisted calls |

## Do Later

- broader city expansion beyond the first 2 primary cities
- broader trade expansion beyond the first 2 primary trades and 1 secondary trade
- comparison-page scale beyond the initial two
- medium-depth nurture sequences beyond the basic autoresponder and simple follow-up prompts
- richer CRM tooling once open pipeline volume justifies it

## Ignore For V1

- paid ads as a primary engine
- nationwide positioning
- broad programmatic SEO at scale
- trying to serve every local trade equally
- generic "post on every social platform" behavior
- overbuilt automation before signal exists

## Sequencing

### First 30-45 days

- outbound and partner motion drive early calls
- local presence and foundational pages support credibility
- proof assets support follow-up and conversion

### Days 31-60

- SEO assets deepen
- comparison and pricing content make the site more persuasive
- proof gets stronger as calls and projects accumulate

### Days 61-90

- expand only the best-performing trade and city
- deepen the partner types and sequences that actually refer
- reduce or kill low-signal motions

## Budget Assumptions

Lean stack only:

- current website and forms stack
- email notifications and basic nurture
- scheduling
- Slack alerts
- Google Sheets or a light CRM

If volume or operational friction rises, upgrade the CRM layer, not the whole system.
