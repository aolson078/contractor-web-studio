# Offer Architecture

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Positioning

This is not generic website design.

Contractor Web Studio sells a local, contractor-specific, trust-focused website service built around how trades businesses actually win work.

## Core Promise

Help owner-led contractors turn a weak digital presence into a cleaner, more credible online presence that makes it easier for local prospects to:

- understand services
- trust the business
- call
- request a quote
- choose them over lower-trust competitors

## Offer Ladder

### Entry Offer

**Website Teardown / Lead Conversion Audit**

Use this to start outbound and partner conversations.

Includes:

- quick review of the current site
- 3-5 conversion blockers
- screenshots and observations
- prioritized fixes
- CTA into a short strategy call

### Flagship Offer

**Custom Contractor Website Build**

Includes:

- custom website build
- mobile-first structure
- clear service architecture
- call and quote CTAs
- proof and review placement
- local service area architecture
- analytics and source tracking
- basic local SEO foundations
- launch support

## Package Mapping

Use the existing site packages as the commercial structure:

### Get Found Online

- best for first site or weak starter site
- use when speed and credibility are the primary need

### Never Miss a Lead

- best for contractors already getting traffic or referrals but losing conversion
- use when response speed, alerts, and follow-up matter

### Grow on Autopilot

- best for contractors ready for city/service depth and stronger automation
- use after fit and budget are clear

## Recommended Add-Ons

- Google Business Profile support
- review request system
- city/service page expansion
- project showcase setup
- case study page creation
- simple email capture and nurture setup

## Messaging Angles

Use these commercial framings repeatedly:

- "People are finding you but not calling."
- "Your workmanship looks better than your website."
- "You rely too much on referrals because your digital presence does not support sales."
- "Your competitors look more established online even when they are not better operators."

## CTA Paths

Every major page and campaign should push into one of these outcomes only:

- Get a Free Quote
- Book a 15-Min Call
- See My Work

Do not create more CTA branches until conversion data shows a real need.
