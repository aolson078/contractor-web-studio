# Proof Engine

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Use only strict, defensible proof that makes outbound, partner referrals, local pages, and proposal conversations easier to convert.

## Approved Proof Sources

- Wolf Lake Masonry live site and screenshots
- approved testimonials already in the repo
- real portfolio entries and mockups grounded in actual examples
- real process descriptions and launch details

## Never Use

- invented ROI claims
- fake logos
- fabricated client outcomes
- "X leads in Y days" claims that cannot be defended
- vague hype language without proof

## Proof Asset Types

### Testimonial snippets

Best for:

- site sections
- outbound follow-up
- partner reassurance
- proposals

### Case studies

Best for:

- portfolio depth
- proposal support
- comparison and pricing pages

### Teardown examples

Best for:

- outbound outreach
- LinkedIn posts
- partner conversations

## Case Study Format

Use this structure:

1. client context
2. visible problem
3. what was built
4. why the structure mattered
5. approved quote or result
6. CTA into quote or call

## Testimonial Capture System

### Ask timing

- within 3-7 days of launch if the client is happy
- again after a visible positive outcome if needed

### Ask format

- short email
- short SMS if the relationship supports it
- direct link when requesting a Google review

Use the templates in `assets/testimonial-request-copy.md`.

## Proof Backlog

Keep a running list of:

- approved testimonials
- screenshots by trade and layout
- before/after teardown observations
- review links and status
- case study candidates

## Conversion Use

Proof should appear:

- on money pages
- on comparison pages
- inside outbound follow-up
- in proposals

Proof should not be buried only in the portfolio.
