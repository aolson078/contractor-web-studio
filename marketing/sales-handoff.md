# Sales Handoff

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Define what happens once a lead books a call so the system remains fast, credible, and low-drag.

Automation ends at booked call. Human selling starts here.

## Pre-Call Checklist

- confirm source, city, and trade
- review the site and audit notes
- review latest messages or referral context
- confirm desired package direction if obvious
- prepare 3 visible blockers to discuss

## Discovery Call Structure

1. confirm what is frustrating them now
2. ask how leads currently come in
3. ask what happens after someone looks them up online
4. walk through the biggest blockers on the current site
5. connect blockers to trust, calls, and quote requests
6. explain the recommended scope
7. set proposal expectations and next steps

## Proposal Rules

- send within 48 hours
- keep the scope tied to the pain discussed on the call
- recommend the smallest package that clearly solves the real problem
- include add-ons only when tied to a concrete outcome

## Follow-Up Rules

- if proposal is sent, set a follow-up date immediately
- if lead is warm but not ready, move to Nurture with a dated next action
- if the lead is not a fit, mark Closed Lost with a reason

## Decision Notes To Capture

- budget range
- urgency
- desired pages or scope
- strongest buying trigger
- strongest hesitation

## Close Quality Standard

Do not chase every lead.

Choose clients who fit the local-first model, respect the process, and value a site that improves trust and conversion.
