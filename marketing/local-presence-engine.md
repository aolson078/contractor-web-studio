# Local Presence Engine

Last updated: 2026-03-17

## Goal

Strengthen local discovery and trust for Contractor Web Studio itself so branded search, local search, referrals, and outbound follow-up all convert better.

## Google Business Profile

Treat the Google Business Profile as part of the lead system, not an afterthought.

### Setup priorities

- category aligned with web designer / internet marketing service
- NC Triad service area clarity
- matching phone, email, and website
- clear description built from the core positioning
- service list aligned with contractor website offers

### Weekly maintenance

- answer or seed common Q&A
- post one useful update or proof item weekly
- refresh photos or screenshots when new proof exists

## Review Loop

Use reviews as a deliberate system.

### Ask moments

- after a positive project launch
- after a fast fix or support win
- after a referral produces a good outcome

### Rules

- make the ask easy
- link directly to the review flow
- keep copy short and human
- log asks and outcomes

## Local Trust Signals

Strengthen these across the site and GBP:

- Winston-Salem / Greensboro relevance
- real local examples
- contractor-specific language
- approved testimonials
- portfolio screenshots

## Local Listings and Mentions

Maintain consistent name, phone, and website across:

- Google Business Profile
- LinkedIn company or founder presence
- key local directories where relevant
- chamber or business group listings if used

## Branded Search Goal

Anyone who searches the business name should quickly see:

- a real website
- local context
- proof
- a clear contact path

That closes referral-driven prospects who look the business up before calling.
